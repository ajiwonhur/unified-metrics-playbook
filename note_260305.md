One of my favorite things to do is go through my saved places on Google Maps. But recently, while revisiting my endless list, I kept hitting the same dead end: “Permanently Closed”. And nothing else.

No recommendation and no redirection. Doesn’t it feel weird?

This is one the clearest idle moments in any product, where the user has already shown intent, now stranded. Google knows I saved a bubble tea spot and it’s gone. Google has everything it needs to say "here are three similar places nearby", because it knows my location, my taste, nearby alternatives. But it just doesn’t.

The best products protect user intent and keep the momentum going. This one's leaving it on the table and worth revisiting.
